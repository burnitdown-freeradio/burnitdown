BURN IT DOWN Free Radio

'Burn It Down' is a community non-commercial free radio, transmitting from New York City.

We are an autonomous, non-hierarchical, multi-cultural collective focusing on amplifying our community's cultural and artistic expression in these challenging times.

We are bringing curated music and live streams from many different backgrounds and genres.

Our programming is free of racism, nationalism, homophobia and sexism.

We would have a monthly program with live music directly from our social center, weekly selected playlists, interviews, and much more.
