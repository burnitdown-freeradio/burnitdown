BURN IT DOWN

Abolitionist Free Radio


'Burn It Down' is a community non-commercial Abolitionist Free Radio, transmitting from somewhere in New York City.

We are an autonomous, non-hierarchical, multi-cultural collective focusing on amplifying our community's cultural and artistic expression in these challenging times.

We are bringing to you curated music-playlists, live shows, podcasts, interviews and much more from different territories, backgrounds, languages and genres.

Our programming is anti-fascist and anti-capitalist, in pro of the abolition of the state, prisons and police.

LOVE MUSIC - HATE FASCISM!
